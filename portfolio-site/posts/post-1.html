Why I prefer small models for prototyping

A short note on iteration speed, cost, and reproducibility when building ML features.

Summary

Small models let you iterate quickly and cheaply. Below are a few practical tips I use when experimenting.

1. Start small

Use smaller architectures, evaluate core behavior, then scale.

2. Instrument heavily

Logs, unit tests, and reproducible seeds are your friends.

3. Costs matter

Lower compute means more runs and faster iteration.
